ABOUT EP-POWER

Established in 2008, EP-POWER provide high performance internal and external power solutions serving the medical, communications, computer display, computer peripheral, computer printer, Point-of-Sales and industrial market. We help our OEMs customer deliver top quality products and systems to their end-customers.

With extensive experiences in power solutions the company designs and develops AC/DC and DC/DC Switching Power Adapter and Switch Mode Power Supply with standard, modified and custom power solutions to meet our client's requirement.

On the demanding power, we provide Programmable AC-DC, AC-AC and DC electronics load to customers who need to have a control over the voltages and currents.

We design the best custom battery pack products that are the most cost effective, environmentally safe and exceed all of the specifications.

And our brands of EMI/EMC/ RFI Filter capabilities help our customers meet international compliance standards and improve the immunity and safety of electrical and electronic equipment.

In the Thermal Processing Solutions; we are specialised in Pyrometers, Thermal Imagers & Systems, Calibration Sources, Fiber Optic Temperature Sensors and the SCR Power Controllers.

EP-POWER designs power solutions for a diverse range of application in many different applications worldwide:

  • Aerospace
  • Industrial
  • Electric Vehicles/Bus
  • Medical Equipment
  • Military
  • Test and Instrumentation
  • LED Lighting
  • Semiconductor Manufacturing
  • Burn-in Chamber
  • Coatings
  • Petrochemical
  • Power Plant
  • Telecom
  • Washing Machines & Home Appliances
